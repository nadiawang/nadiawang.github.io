Careers & College

3/13/2022

Ever since I was 3 years old, I have loved playing piano and entertaining audiences. I hope I can major in music in a performing arts school such as Julliard or the Curtis Institute of Music. Although it seems very unrealistic, this has been one of the goals I have been working very hard toward for years. Another potential major is Computer Science. Because my father also majored in computer science, he encourages me to further explore this field also because it is a good skill to have, since the world is becoming more reliant on technology. Lastly, another potential major would be medicine. Of course, one of the more obvious reasons for going into this field is because doctors, surgeons, and specialists make a lot of money, but I also have an interest in this field. Because my mother passed away from a rare type of cancer (angiosarcoma) a few years ago, I would like to research more on this rare disease.
